# The My Mexico Move App — Master Outline (2025)

A world-class AI-powered companion for planning a move to Mexico and living in Mexico with confidence, clarity, and community.

---

## I. Purpose & Core Philosophy

The My Mexico Move App exists to be:
- A guide in your pocket
- A map through uncertainty
- A translator of culture
- A mentor during transition
- A community for those living between worlds
- A bridge between the United States and Mexico

### Core Promises
1. **Clarity**: No more scattered research, unknown requirements, or unreliable social media gossip.
2. **Companionship**: A sense of being walked through the process—gently, clearly, step by step.
3. **Connection**: To Paul, to experts, to community, to culture, and to purpose.

---

## II. Primary Audiences

### 1. People Planning a Move
**Common concerns:** visas and legal pathways; budget and cost of living; safety; choosing a city; neighborhood research; Spanish level; medical care; real estate and rentals; shipping, pets, and logistics; cultural adaptation.

### 2. People Already Living in Mexico
**Needs:** local services; community; language support; neighborhood insight; safety updates; real estate help; cultural guidance; emotional support; events and integration tools.

---

## III. The App’s Core Function

A personalized, AI-powered relocation assistant that grows with the user over time. It learns the user’s timeline, preferences, anxieties, language level, budget, and visa situation, then provides:
- A customized move plan with step-by-step tasks, deadlines, and reminders
- Personalized recommendations, alerts, and encouragement
- Cultural context and safety advice
- Community pathways and progress tracking

---

## IV. Core Features (Flagship Pillars)

### 1. Intelligent AI Move Planner (“My Mexico Move”)
- Warm onboarding flow that gathers user data
- Personalized relocation roadmap with deadlines, progress tracking, and reminders
- Visa timelines, cost expectations, Spanish learning paths, calendar integration, and printable summaries
- AI-powered insights for city matches, visa pathways, budget breakdowns, cultural readiness, safety guidance, arrival prep, and post-arrival integration

### 2. Destination Comparison Engine
Compare cities by cost of living, rent and utilities, safety, walkability, climate, internet infrastructure, healthcare access, neighborhood types, expat community presence, cultural vibe, festivals, airports and transit, and nature access.

### 3. Safety Dashboard
- City-level summaries with neighborhood indicators and seasonal risks
- Local emergency contacts, embassy and consular info, and city-specific safety tips
- Crowdsourced impressions and AI safety coaching

### 4. Real Estate Finder (Verified, Human-Reviewed)
- Verified listings with rental requirements explained and utility cost expectations
- Apply user profile filters (budget, pets, Spanish level), save and track properties, and add private notes
- Connect with trusted agents; future integrations with iMexico, independent realtors, facilitators, and rental scouts

### 5. Immigration Guidance & Simplified Legal Help
- Visa pathways, costs, timelines, requirements, and red flags
- Facilitator directory, appointment tips, embassy and consulate guides
- Renewal reminders, deadline alerts, and plain-language explanations of government terms

### 6. Travel Planner (for Moving & Exploring)
Itinerary builder, packing lists, airport guidance, shuttle or taxi suggestions, events and activities, city-specific recommendations, cultural etiquette, and where to eat, stay, or socialize.

### 7. Community & Journaling Hub
- A calm, positive space for sharing posts and private journals
- Record Mexico move stories, join interest groups, meet locals and expats, celebrate milestones, and access community Q&A with replies from Paul or verified helpers

### 8. Spanish Learning Companion
- Move-oriented language guidance with survival Spanish, neighborhood vocabulary, apartment terms, and emergency phrases
- Cultural etiquette explanations, AI-powered practice conversations, and progress tracking

### 9. Floating Multi-Agent AI Chat
- Contextual AI that understands move stage, profile, goals, preferences, visa status, budget, and Spanish level
- Users can choose agents: Immigration Agent, Real Estate Agent, Safety Advisor, Cultural Mentor, Spanish Coach, Travel Planner, Emotional Support, or General Assistant

### 10. Booking Portal for Consultations With Paul
In-app scheduling for calls with follow-up sessions via video, audio, or chat that tie directly into the monetization strategy.

---

## V. Monetization Model (Freemium)

- **Free Tier**: Limited destination browsing, basic planner, one AI agent, no community posting, limited move steps.
- **$1 Tier**: Approximately 25–30% access; most features unlocked; AI agent throttled; basic real estate browsing; safety summaries; guided onboarding; limited journaling.
- **$3 Tier**: Nearly everything unlocked; AI agents available; full move planner, safety dashboard, Spanish companion, and travel planner; save unlimited listings; reminders and notifications.
- **$5 Tier (Navigator Pro)**: All AI features, faster responses, downloadable PDFs, priority support, priority booking with Paul, real-time safety alerts, early access to features, and bonus cultural guides.

## VI. Design Language

- **Colors**: Deep Teal (#174A4A), Muted Jade (#2B7D7D), Soft Sage (#D6E4E0), Sand (#F8F2E9), Clay (#C26A4A), Clean White (#FFFFFF)
- **Typography**: Headers in Lora (all caps); body text in Cabin
- **Style**: Spaced, calm, breathable layout with soft shadows and rounded edges; minimalist, Mexican, modern, and human.

---

## VII. User Journey (Full Flow)

### 1. Onboarding
Collects name, intent, budget, timeline, Spanish level, comfort with Mexico, visa status, preferred lifestyles, and must-haves to generate a personalized Move Profile.

### 2. Personalized Dashboard
Tabs include Home, My Mexico Move (full move plan, steps and tasks, progress, calendar sync, deadlines), Explore Cities, Safety Dashboard, Real Estate, Community, Spanish, Travel, Book a Consultation, and Settings/Profile. The dashboard shows a welcome message from Paul, move stage, reminders, saved locations, saved listings, and encouragement messages.

---

## VIII. Technical Architecture

- **Frontend (current)**: React + TypeScript with services/types.ts defining the domain, Gemini-driven AI via geminiService.ts, and full mobile friendliness.
- **Future backend**: Supabase or Firebase for user profiles, move plan storage, real estate storage, and community posts.
- **Future mobile**: React Native (Expo) with shared domain logic and AI services.

---

## IX. AI Architecture (Multi-Agent)

Agents include Immigration, Real Estate, Safety, Culture, Spanish, Travel, Emotional Support, and General. Each has its own system prompt, function-calling abilities, and access to user profiles, while maintaining accuracy boundaries and a warm, calm, grounded personality. The floating chat routes intent, selects the agent, and applies the right context.

---

## X. Long-Term Vision

Guided immigration onboarding; city-specific insider guides; audio lessons; moving checklist exports; housing alerts; community events calendar; Mexico deportee coalition tie-ins; contribution tracking; live stories (“My Mexico Move Journey”); and marketplaces for facilitators and trusted professionals.

This master outline defines what the app is, who it serves, what it does, how it works, what it should look and feel like, how AI fits into everything, how monetization works, and how it grows.
